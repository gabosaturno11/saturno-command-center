Saturn Ring Progress Orb Variations

1. Chapter Mastery Rings

Each ring represents a chapter's completion status. Inner rings are foundational chapters, outer rings are advanced. Ring thickness shows content depth.

2. Skill Integration Orbiter

Central orb represents core strength, with skill satellites orbiting at different speeds based on practice frequency.

3. Philosophy Depth Indicator

Ring opacity and glow intensity represent depth of philosophical understanding. Tap to reveal Gabo quotes at each level.

4. Movement Pattern Compass

Rings divided into segments representing push/pull/squat/hinge/carry/rotate patterns. Segments glow based on current focus.

5. Discipline Ecosystem

Six interconnected rings representing each calisthenics discipline, with connection bridges showing skill transfer between them.

6. Recovery & Adaptation Cycles

Rings pulse and breathe to show training/recovery cycles. Ring color indicates fatigue (red) to supercompensation (green).

7. Mind-Body-Spirit Trinity

Three interlocking rings representing holistic development. Overlap areas show integration points where all three unite.

8. Progression Velocity Tracker

Ring rotation speed indicates learning velocity. Faster rotation = rapid progress, slower = consolidation phase.

Central orb is you, outer rings represent your impact on others. Ring brightness shows teaching/inspiration reach.

10. Elemental Mastery Wheel
